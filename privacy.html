Cumulus Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 16, 2024

At Cumulus, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how James Stewart ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Cumulus app (the "App") or visit https://cumulusbreathing.com/ (the "Website"). By using the App or Website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information I Collect

a. Information from the App:

When you use the App, I may collect:

  • Sign in with Apple: Your email address (either the real email or an anonymous relay email based on your choice during sign-up).
  • App Usage Data: Non-personal information, such as session length, frequency of use, and interactions with features in the App.
  • App Preferences: Settings or preferences you adjust within the App (e.g., custom breathing intervals).

b. Information from the Website:

When you visit the Website, I may collect:

  • Non-Personal Data: Information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior. This is collected through cookies or other tracking technologies to help me understand how users interact with the Website.
  • Cookies: The Website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and track aggregate user behavior. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may impact the functionality of the Website.

I do not collect any additional personal information unless you choose to provide it, such as by contacting me directly through a form or subscribing to an email list.

2. How I Use Your Information

The information I collect from the App and Website is used to:

  • Operate and Improve the App and Website: Non-personal data is used to ensure functionality and improve features over time.
  • Email Communication: Your email address (if provided) may be used for important communications regarding the App or updates to this Privacy Policy.
  • Customizing Your Experience: App preferences are stored to provide a personalized experience each time you use the App.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

I do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, I may share your data in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: Trusted third-party service providers who assist in the operation of the App or Website (e.g., hosting platforms, analytics services) may have access to limited information, but only as necessary to perform their services.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, I may disclose your information in response to legal obligations such as subpoenas or court orders.

4. Cookies and Analytics

  • Cookies on the Website: Cookies are small text files placed on your device to collect information about your activity on the Website. These cookies help me analyze web traffic and improve the user experience.
  • Google Analytics: I may use Google Analytics to collect information about how users interact with the Website. This data is anonymous and does not personally identify you.
    • If you prefer, you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Data Security

I take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Update: You can request access to the personal information I hold about you and update or correct it as needed.
  • Delete: You can request the deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting me.
  • Opt-Out: If you are receiving marketing emails, you can unsubscribe at any time by following the instructions in those emails.

7. Children's Privacy

The App and Website are not intended for children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If I become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, I will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in how I collect or use your information. If I make material changes, I will notify you through the App or Website. Continued use of the App or Website after such updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you would like to exercise your data rights, please contact me at:
